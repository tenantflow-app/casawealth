Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 March 2026  ·  CasaWealth Pty Ltd

This policy applies to CasaWealth Pty Ltd (ABN to be inserted), operating as CasaWealth and the CasaFlow platform. We are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that is necessary to provide our property management and real estate services. This may include:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and postal address
  • Identity documents required under Queensland tenancy legislation (RTRA Act 2008)
  • Financial information such as bank account details for rental payments and disbursements
  • Property details, lease agreements, and maintenance records
  • Communications between you and CasaWealth or CasaFlow
  • Usage data from the CasaFlow platform (browser type, pages visited, actions taken)

2. How We Collect Information

We collect personal information:

  • Directly from you when you complete forms, apply for a tenancy, or contact us
  • From tenancy application platforms, credit check services, or referral agencies
  • Automatically through the CasaFlow platform via cookies and usage logs
  • From publicly available sources such as the Queensland land registry

3. How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used to:

  • Manage tenancy applications, leases, and property maintenance
  • Process rental payments and owner disbursements
  • Meet our obligations under the RTRA Act 2008 and other Queensland legislation
  • Communicate with you about your property or tenancy
  • Improve and operate the CasaFlow platform
  • Send relevant service updates, inspection notices, and renewal reminders

We will not use your personal information for direct marketing without your consent, and you may opt out at any time.

4. Sharing Your Information

We may share your personal information with:

  • Property owners and landlords in relation to your tenancy
  • Licensed tradespeople and contractors for approved maintenance
  • Credit reporting bodies for tenancy applications (with your consent)
  • QCAT, the RTA, or other regulatory bodies where required by law
  • Third-party service providers who assist us in operating the CasaFlow platform (e.g. cloud hosting, payment processors) — all bound by confidentiality agreements

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Storage and Security

Personal information is stored securely on Australian-based servers and within the CasaFlow platform infrastructure. We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption at rest and in transit, role-based access controls, and regular security reviews.

We retain personal information only for as long as required by law or for the purpose for which it was collected. Tenancy records are retained for a minimum of 7 years in accordance with Queensland legislation.

6. Cookies

The CasaWealth website and CasaFlow platform use cookies to improve your experience. You may disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not function correctly. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

7. Access and Correction

You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you, and to request corrections if it is inaccurate. To make a request, contact us at hello@casawealth.com.au. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Complaints

If you believe we have breached the Australian Privacy Principles, please contact us first at hello@casawealth.com.au. If you are not satisfied with our response, you may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version will always be available at casawealth.com.au/privacy-policy. Continued use of our services following any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
